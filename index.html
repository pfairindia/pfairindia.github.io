About Air India Virtual

Air India Virtual is a dedicated community for flight simulation enthusiasts who wish to experience the thrill of flying for India's flag carrier, Air India. We strive to provide a realistic and engaging virtual airline experience, offering a vast route network, regular events, and a supportive community for pilots of all skill levels.

Our mission is to foster a passion for aviation, promote learning, and create a fun and challenging environment where virtual pilots can hone their skills and share their love for flying.
